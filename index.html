📥 How to get your eBird data:

  1. Go to eBird's request page: ebird.org/downloadMyData.
  2. Click Request Data Transfer. eBird will package your lifetime checklist observations (takes a few hours to a day).
  3. Once you receive the eBird email, download the zip archive.
  4. Drag & drop the zip file or CSV directly into the box above to analyze!
